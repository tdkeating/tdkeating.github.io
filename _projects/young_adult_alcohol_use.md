University of Washington, Seattle

This is an independent research study exploring the association between self-reported levels of depression and alcohol use in young adults. The study uses ordinal logistic regression in the primary analysis. It is based on data from the [National Longitudinal Survey of Youth (1997)](https://www.bls.gov/nls/nlsy97.htm).
