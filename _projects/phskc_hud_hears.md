Public Health, Seattle and King County (PHSKC)

This is an ongoing research study at PHSKC in the Assessment, Policy Development, and Evaluation Unit on a Housing and Urban Development- Health, Employment, and Residential Stability project. The project involves designing and implementing a study to evaluate how exits from public housing assistance programs are associated with housing stability and economic outcomes.
